Tracing the Path.

We didn't start in a boardroom. We started on the trail, in the quiet hours before dawn when the only sound was the rhythm of footsteps against the earth. Trace was born from a simple promise: to design gear that doesn't just endure the elements, but connects you to them.

Our Philosophy

Trace is a dedicated exploration of performance and design. We build technical outerwear and footwear that function as a natural extension of the athlete. By marrying sustainable innovations with a refined, modern aesthetic, we co-create gear that stands up to the demands of elite performance while maintaining a minimal footprint on the landscapes we run.

Our Collaborations

Our Core Elements

Sustainable Performance
We source recycled, organic, and biodegradable fabrics to ensure our footprint on the earth is as light as our shoes. Running should leave nothing but tracks behind.
Collaborative Spirit
We don't build in isolation. By collaborating with running pioneers like ASICS, HOKA, and On Running, we fuse alpine tech, urban durability, and elite athletic designs.
Community in Motion
We support grassroots running clubs, community workshops, and trail-maintenance programs, creating an open ecosystem where everyone is invited to run.
